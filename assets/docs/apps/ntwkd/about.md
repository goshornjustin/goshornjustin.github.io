# NTWKD App

## About
- A mobile contact card application that makes networking easy. Ntwkd allows you to create an all-in-one contact card and share your card via a QR code. Create up to five contact cards for personal or business use. Create custom Qr codes, Custom contact cards and more. Add all your socials, websites or other contact details. Export contacts for more efficient networking and time management.


### Features
    - Custom Qr Codes
    - Custom Contact Cards
    - Unlimited contacts
    - Contact card sharing,
    - Contact Card Exporting
    - Up to five personalized contact cards
