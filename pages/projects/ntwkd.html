NTWKD — Contact Card App

Digital networking cards with custom QR codes — live on iOS & Android

Overview

NTWKD is a professional networking app built for The Big 3 LLC that lets users create up to five unique digital contact cards, each with its own custom QR code for instant sharing. It is fully launched and actively maintained on both the App Store and Google Play with a live user base.

The Challenge

Paper business cards are easy to lose and impossible to update. The goal was a frictionless way to share rich, up-to-date contact details in person or remotely, while letting one person keep separate cards for different contexts — work, side projects, social.

The Solution

NTWKD gives each user up to five distinct cards, each generating a scannable QR code that opens the card instantly. Cards support photos, social links, and website integration, and can export straight into the phone's native contacts. Subscriptions and secure payments are handled in-app, with Firebase backing auth and data and Method Channels bridging to native platform features.

Key Features

  • Create and customize up to 5 unique contact cards
  • Generate custom QR codes for easy sharing
  • Social media and website integration
  • Photo integration for contact cards
  • Export contacts to phone contacts
  • Subscription model with secure payments

Tech Stack
