My Professional Journey

My journey in mobile development began during my studies at Penn State University, where I pursued a Bachelor of Science in Information Systems with a focus on Application Development. This foundation gave me a solid understanding of software engineering principles and data analysis.

At The Big 3 LLC, I've had the incredible opportunity to work on production mobile applications, including NTWKD, which is currently live on both the App Store and Google Play Store. This experience has taught me the importance of writing clean, maintainable code and the intricacies of shipping real-world applications to thousands of users.

What I love most about mobile development is the intersection of technology and user experience. Every line of code I write is ultimately about creating something that makes people's lives better or more enjoyable. Whether it's implementing smooth animations, integrating complex APIs, or ensuring offline functionality works seamlessly, I'm always thinking about the end user.
